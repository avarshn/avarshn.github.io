About Me

Hey there! I’m Abhishek Varshney, a recent CS grad from Boston University, deeply curious about how machine learning can move the needle in areas that actually matter — like healthcare, accessibility, and real-world decision-making. I didn’t start in healthcare, but that’s where I’ve found some of the most rewarding problems. At BU, I spent the past year building AI models to detect early signs of dementia using both voice and language data — the kind of project that makes you realize how much more AI can be than just code and metrics. I'm currently looking for Machine Learning Engineer roles where I can design, develop and deploy scalable ML systems — ideally on mission-driven ML products, research-backed pipelines, or systems where AI isn’t just a buzzword but a tool to genuinely improve people’s lives. If that sounds like your team, I’d love to connect. Outside of work, I’m either on a badminton court or buried in a Kaggle competition.
